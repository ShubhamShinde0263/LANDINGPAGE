About SAE - Sinhgad Academy of Engineering

Established in 2005, SAE - Sinhgad Academy of Engineering is a premier Institute located in Maharashtra. Institute offers Degree courses including 8 UG and 5 PG courses. These programs are offerred in Full Time mode. SAE - Sinhgad Academy of Engineering is a premier institute for B.E. / B.Tech, M.E./M.Tech courses. Candidate can pursue in multiple range of courses like B.E. / B.Tech, M.E./M.Tech with the stream of Engineering. Over the period, SAE - Sinhgad Academy of Engineering has gained expertize through its trained and experienced faculty in the fields of Computer Science Engineering, Electronics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Information Technology, Civil Engineering, Automobile Engineering, VLSI Design. Institue offered courses that are approved by AICTE. College offered courses with intake of 861 candidates . Courses offered by the Institute are in the fee range of INR 190,000-344,888 for a total of 861 candidates. SAE - Sinhgad Academy of Engineering offers good infrastructure like Auditorium, Boys Hostel, Cafeteria, Convenience Store, Girls Hostel, Gym, Hospital / Medical Facilities, Hostel, Labs, Library, Shuttle Service, Sports Complex, Wi-Fi Campus to students.
